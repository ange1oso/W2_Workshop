# Requirements and Conceptual Model
1 a) Angel Osorio, Ceyhun Ugur, Paul Lopez, Sultan Simsek, David Ocampo
1 b) The "end user" is the business owner, Lana, and she would be tracking the data of her business. The categories would be Time, Cost, Client( Phone number and name)
1 c) 
Client- Client ID, Name, Phone Number, Location, Age, # of Dogs/Client,
Dog- Dog ID, Dog breed, Age of dog, Dog name, 
Service- Duration of service, time of service, Cost per service

2 a) The main entities would be Client, Dog, and Service.
2 b) The attributes of the entities 
For Client- Client ID, Name, Phone Number, Location, Age, # of Dogs/Client,
For Dog- Client ID, Dog ID, Dog breed, Age of dog, Dog name, 
For Service- Client ID, Dog ID, Duration of service, time of service, Cost per service
2 c) Client ID and Dog ID to create the relationship between the tables. 
2 d) There are many-to-many relationships between the client and the service table.
